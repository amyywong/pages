WHO ARE WE

The New School Art Gallery is an art gallery created to encourage aspiring artists at The New School to share their art with the world.

Every month a theme/topic will be presented and we encourage all students to submit one piece of their artwork inspired by that topic for it to be displayed.

There is limited space therefore not everyone will get a spot. The changing themes of the gallery will bring different knowledge and experiences into the room.

This gallery strives to connect students, professors, tourists together to appreciate the creativity and talent we have at The New School.

This is also an excellent opportunity for hidden talent to be discovered by the city. It is time for such talented artists to come out of hiding.

This gallery welcomes all forms of visual art: paintings, drawings, sculptures, digital designs, photography, etc. Everyone is encouraged to join and help the community of young artists grow.
